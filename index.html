The Solution-First Trap vs. The MADpm Way

We have spent years building answers to questions nobody was asking. It's time to shift the paradigm.

THE REALITY TODAY

Solution-First Chaos

  • ❌
    Scatterbrained Observations: Frustrations are lost in Reddit threads, Twitter rants, and forgotten Notion pages.
  • ❌
    Anecdotal Validation: Validation is based on a single client's feedback or a founder's loudest bias.
  • ❌
    Blind Iterations: Teams build high-fidelity MVPs only to discover the core problem doesn't exist or isn't severe.
  • ❌
    Output-Only Portfolios: Early professionals show "Spotify Clones" instead of demonstrating how they observe and evaluate issues.
THE MADPM SOLUTION

Problem-First Clarity

  • ✅
    Structured Capture: Every problem statement defines Context, Triggers, Affected Cohorts, and Existing Alternatives.
  • ✅
    Community Signal Processing: Problems are upvoted by severity, critiqued, and cross-referenced with domain experts.
  • ✅
    Pre-validated Demand: Startups browse curated, qualified problem sets to find what users actually struggle with.
  • ✅
    Verified Proof of Thinking: Solvers build public portfolios showcasing high-scoring problem canvases and reviews.
